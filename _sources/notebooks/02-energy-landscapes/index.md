# Volume II — Energy Landscapes & Optimization

The potential energy surface is the object on which all of molecular and materials
modelling plays out. This volume builds one explicitly for Lennard-Jones clusters,
explores its rugged structure of local minima, and develops the optimization
methods that find the deepest of them: local quasi-Newton relaxation, global
basin-hopping, and the order parameters that classify the structures found.
